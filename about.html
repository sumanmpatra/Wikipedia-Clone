About Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia, created and edited by volunteers around the world. It is hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation, a non-profit organization.

Launched on January 15, 2001, by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger, Wikipedia is now one of the largest and most popular general reference works on the internet.

Wikipedia's content is written collaboratively by largely anonymous volunteers who write without pay, and its articles can be edited by anyone with internet access. This makes it different from most other encyclopedias.

Wikipedia exists in multiple languages and has become one of the most visited websites globally, helping users gather knowledge about a wide range of topics.
